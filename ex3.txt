Remove execute permission for everybody:
chmod 666 _ex3.txt
Grant all permissions to owner and others:
chmod 707 _ex3.txt

Let r - permission for read
    w - permission for write
    x - permission for execute

660 - permission for r+w to owner and group;

775 - permission for r+w+x to owner and group and
      permissionfor r+x for others;

777 - permission for r+w+x to owner, group and others;
